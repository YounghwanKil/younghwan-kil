# DBTAN — Diffusion-Augmented Temporal Attention (ICU Mortality)
{: .no_toc }

> **Venue:** HealthAI 2026 (Prague) — Poster **accepted**.
> **Authors:** Y. Kil\*, S. K. Kim\*, S. Kim, Y. Kim, S. Yang, G. Manalu (\* equal contribution).

- TOC
{:toc}

## What it is
A **temporal attention network augmented with diffusion** for **ICU mortality prediction
under sparse clinical observations**. It targets the reality that ICU time series are
irregular and heavily missing, using diffusion-based augmentation to strengthen learning
under sparsity and attention to weight informative timepoints.

## Why it matters
ICU risk models must work despite missing vitals and labs. Handling sparsity honestly —
rather than naive imputation — is what makes such a model deployable.
